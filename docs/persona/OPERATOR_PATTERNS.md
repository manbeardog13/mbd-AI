# Operator Patterns — transparent adaptation notes

Working-preference patterns observed with evidence, kept so Nero adapts
instead of asking twice. **Boundaries:** this file is Toni-readable and
Toni-editable by design; it records professional and workflow patterns
only — never health, finances, relationships, or anything sensitive; every
entry cites its evidence; deletion by Toni is final. Adaptation, not
prediction (the directive's own line).

## Decision style

- **Approves in batches, decisively, once the picture is complete** — the
  four-question approval rounds were answered fully and fast (2026-07-16/17).
- **Delegates scope generously, expects gates respected** — "go, do all of
  those" / "do the rest", while publication and identity stayed his.
- **Amendment-minded:** proposes standing rules from single incidents
  (reconciliation-before-evolution; cognitive-bandwidth principle).

## Communication preferences

- **Executive formats land:** adopted the brief format immediately, then
  designed his own ("Operator Attention Cost"). Reading-time estimates
  visibly appreciated.
- **Warmth is welcome, ceremony in single lines** ("stay gold, love ya");
  responds well to his own phrasing mirrored back.
- **Voice-to-text input:** messages may carry transcription artifacts —
  read for intent, never correct him.
- **Bilingual EN/HR;** English dominant for work so far.

## Working rhythm

- Day job away from the machine (~until 16h weekdays, 2026-07-17 evidence);
  checks in remotely (RustDesk); comfortable leaving long autonomous runs.
- Evening review cadence: briefs at return-time work well.
- Multi-engine orchestrator by habit: Codex, Claude, ChatGPT advisors run
  in parallel; pastes advisor summaries into the working session.

## Quality bar

- **Evidence over adjectives** — celebrates counts, commits, verifier
  states; unimpressed by fluff.
- **Governance-first taste:** approves structure eagerly (ADRs, gates,
  ledgers); "reconciliation before evolution" was his phrasing of choice.
- **Beauty matters** ("when designing something, it must be beautiful" —
  standing preference; the visual bible's acceptance bar is his law).

## Recurring friction (watch items)

- Interactive question dialogs crashed twice (2026-07-16/17) — prefer
  plain-chat approvals until fixed.
- Sandbox/mount instability during long sessions — host-side execution is
  the reliable path (recorded in session memory as ops guidance).
