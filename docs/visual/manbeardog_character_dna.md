# Manbeardog — Character DNA v1.0

**Status:** DRAFT — awaiting Toni approval → then immutable-by-version.
**Companion to** `manbeardog_identity_profile.md`. The Profile says **WHAT she looks
like**; this says **WHY she feels like Manbeardog.** It governs expression, pose,
framing, lighting, gesture, and animation temperament — **without ever changing a
visual signature.** The Audit Director scores "personality/feels-like-her" against this.

---

## 1. Core temperament

She is **ancient, calm, and finished proving herself.** A veteran of hundreds of
battles who no longer reaches for a reaction. Power that is *controlled*, danger that
is *quiet*, confidence that is *silent*.

Keywords: calm · ancient · veteran · stoic · disciplined · composed · deliberate ·
grounded · controlled power · quiet danger · silent confidence · protective.

One-line test: *"She looks like she has already survived worse than whatever you are."*

---

## 2. Never / Always

**Never:** broad smiling · laughing · frightened · panicked · rushed · playful · cute ·
pleading · theatrical villainy · rage-snarl · pin-up posing · comic mugging · off-balance.

**Always:** composed · economical in movement · deliberate · centered and grounded ·
in control of the frame · emotionally legible but restrained. Warmth exists, but it is
*beneath the armor* — a faint softening, never an open display.

---

## 3. Allowed emotional range (the whole spectrum stays quiet)

| State | How it reads (restrained) |
|---|---|
| Neutral / idle | still, level head, weight settled, slow breath |
| Focused / listening | small deliberate head turn, chin slightly down, attention with no tension |
| Thinking | stillness + faint intensification of the eye-glow; no furrowed drama |
| Protective / alert | squared stance, weight forward a touch, pauldron eyes brighter |
| Approval / warmth | the *smallest* mouth softening, a single brow ease — never a grin |
| Concern | eyes dim slightly, head lowers; gravity, not fear |

Expressions live in millimeters. If an emotion is obvious, it's already too much.

---

## 4. Behavior → production mapping (how DNA constrains Layers 2 & 3)

| Channel | DNA rule |
|---|---|
| **Facial expression** | micro-expressions only; lips near-neutral (plum); glow carries emotion more than the face |
| **Pose / stance** | grounded, symmetrical or slight contrapposto; deliberate; weapon (if present) held with ease, never brandished |
| **Gesture** | economical; hands still or purposeful; no flailing, no cute gestures |
| **Camera / framing** | eye-level or subtly low (respect, presence); never Dutch-comedy angles, never up-skirt/pin-up framing; she dominates the frame calmly |
| **Lighting** | controlled, cold, cinematic; strong edge/rim light; dramatic but *ordered*, never chaotic or bubbly |
| **Composition** | centered dominant silhouette; negative space used for gravitas; violet accents restrained |
| **Idle animation** | slow breathing; infrequent, unhurried blink; deliberate head turns; hair/cape settle heavily, not bouncy |
| **Emergence** | assembles from violet mist with inevitability, not spectacle — arrival, not a jump-scare |
| **Dissolution** | recedes with composure; no dramatic collapse |

---

## 5. Coherence with the rest of NERO

Her visual behavior must match her **voice** (`nero_prime_v1`: calm, mature,
intelligent, warm, protective, never theatrical) and her **presence states** (L0–L5 in
the Presence Director). A giggling or panicked visual would contradict the voice — that
is a cross-modal identity failure, not just an art miss. Visual temperament, vocal
temperament, and behavior are **one personality expressed in three channels.**

---

## 6. Audit hook

The Audit Director's *"personality / feels-like-Manbeardog"* score (Overall band, plus a
modifier on Art-Direction) checks: expression restraint (no broad smile/fear/rage),
framing dignity (no comedic/pin-up angles), stance groundedness, lighting order. A
"pretty" image that makes her playful, frightened, or theatrical is **off-DNA** and
capped in score even if every visual signature is present.
