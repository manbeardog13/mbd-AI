# Primary research basis

EGCSE is an engineering synthesis, not a claim of reproducing one paper or a
secret algorithm.

- **Agentic Context Engineering (ACE, 2025/2026):** evolves structured playbooks
  through generation, reflection, and curation while guarding against context
  collapse. <https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.04618>
- **MemSkill (2026):** treats memory operations as selectable, evolvable skills
  with a controller, executor, and designer that revises hard cases.
  <https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.02474>
- **MUSE-Autoskill (2026):** manages skills through creation, memory, evaluation,
  reuse, and refinement as a lifecycle. <https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27366>
- **XSkill (2026):** combines reusable action-level experience with structured
  task-level skills for multimodal continual learning without parameter updates.
  <https://openreview.net/forum?id=AjP1yvCyoG>
- **Contextual Experience Replay (2024/2025):** retrieves and synthesizes relevant
  trajectory experience into the active context. <https://openreview.net/forum?id=RXvFK5dnpz>
- **CLIN (2024):** maintains causal abstractions rather than isolated hints for
  continual task adaptation. <https://openreview.net/forum?id=xS6zx1aBI9>
- **Latency-Quality Routing (2026):** uses contextual bandit routing and
  multiplicative latency-quality matching for equivalent tool providers.
  <https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14241>
- **Self-Generated In-Context Examples (2025):** curates successful trajectories
  by empirical utility for future tasks. <https://openreview.net/forum?id=WdL3O58gde>

The implementation deliberately adds stricter promotion, privacy, permission,
quarantine, and rollback gates than an unconstrained self-evolving research
prototype.
