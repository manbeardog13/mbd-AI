DEBATE CC - CODEX / CLAUDE REVIEW PROTOCOL

1. Debate only a concrete task digest, acceptance criteria, tools, risks, and
   grader. Do not exchange hidden reasoning or chain-of-thought.
2. Maximum three rounds. Each host may submit one decision per round:
   APPROVE, REVISE, or BLOCK.
3. Agreement requires APPROVE from Codex and Claude on the identical current
   task digest. No response is not agreement.
4. After round three without agreement, stop and ask Toni. Do not loop.
5. After execution, each host audits independently. Tests and source evidence
   outrank agreement or confidence.
6. A saved edit creates a durable signal only while the opt-in watcher is
   running. Inactive hosted sessions are notified on their next checked turn.
